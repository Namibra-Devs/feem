Advancing Empowerment For Women & Girls

Female Empowerment Initiative is an organization (FEEM) with the aim of causing a positive impact in society through women both the young and old. We have identified that, to solve society's numerous problems of which poverty is one, there is the need to invest in women especially in their education.
