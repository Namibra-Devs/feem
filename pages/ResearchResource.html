Resources & Research

Welcome to our resource page. We believe that access to reliable information and resources is essential for achieving our mission of improving the health and well-being of women and girls around the Central and upper east regions. On this page, you'll find a curated list of resources related to women's health, including information on sexual and reproductive health. We hope that these resources will be helpful to you and that you'll share them with others who may benefit from this information.

Researches

Gender Based Violence

Gender-based violence (GBV) refers to harmful acts directed at individuals based on their gender. It is a pervasive violation of human rights and a significant public health issue that affects individuals of all genders though women and girls are disproportionately affected.

Read More

Sexual Health

Our organization provides essential sexual health education to empower girls with the knowledge to make informed decisions about their bodies. We offer workshops and resources on safe practices, consent, and reproductive health, aiming to reduce stigma and promote healthy relationships. By prioritizing sexual health, we create a supportive environment for young girls to thrive.

Read More

Menstrual Hygiene

We provide girls with vital menstrual hygiene education, ensuring they understand how to manage their periods with confidence and dignity. Our programs distribute hygiene products and offer guidance on maintaining cleanliness during menstruation. By promoting menstrual health, we aim to break taboos and support girls' well-being, enabling them to stay in school and participate fully in their communities.

Read More

Building Self Esteem

Our programs focus on empowering girls to recognize their worth and build confidence. Through mentorship, workshops, and peer support, we help girls develop a positive self-image, overcome societal pressures, and embrace their strengths. By fostering self-esteem, we aim to equip girls with the resilience and belief in themselves needed to pursue their dreams and make a lasting impact.
