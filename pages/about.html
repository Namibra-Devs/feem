Know About Us

To make the lives of People & the community better .

OverAll Goal Of Our Organization

To make the lives of people in communities, societies and nations better, through the empowerment of women and girls.

►

Our Mission

Provision of livelihood to women and girls

To empower women and girls on leadership, sexual and reproductive health, sexual violence, provision of livelihood to vulnerable women and girls and sustain the education of vulnerable girls leading to informed decisions among women and girls.

Our Vision

Enable woman take up leadership roles

To reach out to women and girls to make informed decisions to enable them take up leadership roles in every aspect of the economy, both in Ghana and beyond.
